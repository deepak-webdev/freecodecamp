B. R. Ambedkar

Here's is a time line of B. R. Ambedkar :

  • 1891 - Born in the town and military cantonment of Mhow in the Central Provinces (now in Madhya Pradesh)
  • 1897 -Ambedkar's family moved to Mumbai where Ambedkar became the only untouchable enrolled at Elphinstone High School
  • 1906 -marriage with a nine-year-old girl, Ramabai.
  • 1912 -he obtained his degree in economics and political science from Bombay University.
  • 1935 -Ambedkar was appointed principal of the Government Law College, Bombay.
  • 1948 -Death due to diabetes.
